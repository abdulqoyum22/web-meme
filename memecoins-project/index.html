THE RUNNER NARRATIVE

The Runner memecoin is based on a viral video of a wolf-like dog sprinting away from security guards. The clip was edited with dramatic, Olympic-style camera cuts that made the chase look like a serious race, and it spread quickly on X. Because of how fast the video trended, the Solana token inspired by it was created within hours.
